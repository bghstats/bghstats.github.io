BGH Guild League Statistics

The Dashboard above has 3 pages, use the arrows at the bottom to navigate the report. Please note that this is subset of the Season 1 Guild league games. In total the guild played 224 matches, 34 matches in the 15-man format, and 190 matches in the 10-man format. The report above was generated using 150 out of the 190 total matches since Pearl Abyss changed the format to 10 man. The missing 21% of matches were left out due to OCR application issues, should be able to fix this for season 2. Don't take any of the stat's to seriously, some classes will perform better in the format and gear is a large factor.
